Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

At CoPair, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Couple Data: Information about your partner connection and shared activities.
  • User Content: Moods, calendar events, and messages you create.
  • Location Data: If you enable location sharing, we collect your precise location to share with your partner.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
  • Facilitate connection and data sharing between you and your partner.
  • Send you technical notices and support messages.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your information with:

  • Your Partner: As the core function of the App, your connected partner sees the data you choose to share.
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services (like Firebase) to host our infrastructure.

4. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Manual Account Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data. You can initiate this request directly within the App under "Settings > Account & Profile".

Please Note: To ensure the safety and integrity of shared couple data, account deletion is a manual process performed by our administrative team. Once you submit a request, we will verify the request and permanently delete your data from our systems. This process is irreversible.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@copair.app.
